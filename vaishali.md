
# Keshavs.com 6-Month Marketing Plan

## Overview
Strategic marketing initiative for a technical writing agency focusing on brand awareness, client acquisition, and thought leadership.

---

## Month 1-2: Foundation & Positioning

### Content Marketing
- Launch blog with 2-3 technical writing guides/month
- Create case studies showcasing past projects
- Develop downloadable resource library (style guides, templates)

### Social Media
- Establish LinkedIn presence with company updates
- Share industry insights and writing tips
- Engage with technical writing communities

### PR Strategy
- Identify 5-10 target tech publications (Dev.to, TechCrunch, CSS-Tricks)
- Draft press release introducing agency expertise
- Reach out to industry journalists

---

## Month 3-4: Authority Building

### Thought Leadership
- Guest post on 2-3 industry publications
- Host webinar: "Technical Writing Best Practices"
- Publish whitepaper on documentation standards

### Partnerships
- Collaborate with complementary agencies
- Join technical writing associations

### PR Outreach
- Target podcasts for founder interviews
- Pitch feature stories to tech blogs

---

## Month 5-6: Conversion & Growth

### Lead Generation
- Launch targeted LinkedIn/Google ads
- Implement email nurture campaigns
- Offer free technical writing audit

### Analytics & Optimization
- Track ROI across channels
- Refine messaging based on performance
- Plan Q2 strategy

---

## Success Metrics
- Website traffic growth
- Lead quality and conversion rate
- Social engagement rate
- PR mention impressions


## 15-Day Campaign Plan

### Day 1-3: Awareness Phase
- **Message:** "Discover the power of effective technical writing! Join us as we share insights and resources."
- **Channel:** Social Media (LinkedIn, Twitter)

### Day 4-6: Engagement Phase
- **Message:** "Have questions about technical writing? Our experts are here to help! Comment below or send us a message."
- **Channel:** Blog Post & Social Media

### Day 7-9: Educational Phase
- **Message:** "Check out our latest blog on 'Top 5 Technical Writing Tips' and elevate your documentation skills!"
- **Channel:** Email Newsletter & Blog

### Day 10-12: Conversion Phase
- **Message:** "Ready to enhance your technical documentation? Sign up for a free audit today!"
- **Channel:** Landing Page & Email Campaign

### Day 13-15: Follow-Up Phase
- **Message:** "Thank you for engaging with us! Stay tuned for more resources and insights on technical writing."
- **Channel:** Social Media & Email
